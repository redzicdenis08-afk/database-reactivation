# Hook Angle Reference

Adoracle detects these hook angles in ad headlines and body copy.

## Pain

Triggers by calling out a specific frustration or problem.

**Signals:** `stop wasting`, `are you tired of`, `the problem with`,
`why most X fail`, `mistake`, `wrong`, `broken`

**Example:** *Stop Wasting Money on Ads That Don't Convert*

## Transformation

Promises a before/after result.

**Signals:** `went from`, `increased by`, `turned X into Y`,
`how we`, `results`, `% improvement`

**Example:** *This Single Fix Increased Booked Jobs by 34%*

## Social proof

Uses numbers, testimonials, or community size.

**Signals:** `X customers`, `X founders`, `join`, `trusted by`,
`reviews`, `rated`

**Example:** *Join 2,000+ founders who use...*

## Scarcity

Creates urgency through limited availability or time.

**Signals:** `limited`, `only X left`, `this week`, `expires`,
`last chance`, `closing soon`

**Example:** *Limited: 3 Free Ad Audits This Week*

## Authority

Signals expertise or data-backed credibility.

**Signals:** `we analyzed`, `study`, `#1`, `expert`,
`proven`, `certified`

**Example:** *We analyzed 500 campaigns...*
